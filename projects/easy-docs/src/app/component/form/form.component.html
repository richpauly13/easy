Form

import { FormModule } from 'easy-framework';

Basic forms are styled with .field-group, .form-label, and .form-field classes.

Note: Forms will have a unique id, a unique for, role, and .pad-* class attributes added, if not present.

Fieldset

A fieldset is optionally styled with a .fieldset class and has an optional <legend> tag as the first child.

A Fieldset is used to group similar fields together both visually and for assitive technologies.

Contact Information

Form Label

Form labels are styled with a .form-label class.

Form Field

Form fields are styled with a .form-field class.  Different styles are applied based on the form field.

Demographics

Form Group Inline

Inline form groups are styled with .form-group-inline class.

Field Group

Field groups are styled with a .field-group class.

Checkbox/Radio Group

Checkbox and radio fields are styled with a .checkbox-group or .radio-group and .checkbox-label or .radio-label classes.
