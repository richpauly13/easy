Text

Text size, alignment, and transform can be easily styled with .text-* classes.

Text Size

Text size is styled with a .text-[xs || sm || md || lg || xl] class.

extra small (.75rem/12px)

small (.875rem/14px)

default (1rem/16px)

medium (1.125rem/18px)

large (1.5rem/24px)

extra large (2.25rem/36px)
