Alerts are styled with an .alert-[bad || good || info || warn] class.

Note: The aria role="alert", tabindex="-1", and a unique aria-labelledby and id will be automatically assigned to the alert.

Alerts are closed by adding a .close class.

Note: The aria role="alertdialog" will be automatically assigned to an alert with a .close class.
