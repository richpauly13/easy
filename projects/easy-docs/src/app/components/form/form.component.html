Basic forms are styled with .field-group, .form-label, and .form-field classes.

Form Label

Form labels are styled with a .form-label class.

Form Field

Form fields are styled with a .form-field class.  Different styles are applied based on the form field.

Fieldset

Fieldsets are styled with a .fieldset class and have a <legend> tag as the first child.

Form Group

Form groups are styled with a .form-group class for vertical fields and .form-group-inline for horizontal fields.

Field Group

Field groups are styled with a .field-group class.

Checkbox/Radio Group

Checkbox and radio fields are grouped with a .checkbox-group or .radio-group and .checkbox-label or .radio-label classes.
